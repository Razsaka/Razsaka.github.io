About me

My name is Razak Saka. I have more than 10 years work experience across Accounting and Finance environment in the Financial Service industry. I earned a Bachelors degree in Accounting and MBA from Baldwin-Wallace University. I had always been interested in Information Technology. My initial focus was on Business Intelligence and Financial and Data Analytics. I was accepted in the Harvard Business Analytics Graduate Certificate Program. The program is designed to leverage data analytics tools like predictive analysis, artificial intelligence and machine learning to solve business challenges to ensure sound data-driven decision-making.

A friend suggested that I consider a coding bootcamp program. He believes a bootcamp program would provide a good coding foundation and strengthen technical and problem-solving skills to support transition to different IT careers within a reasonably short amount of time. I decided to enroll in the "We Can Code It" Software Developer bootcamp program.
